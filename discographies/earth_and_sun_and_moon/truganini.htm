From the 1993 album "Earth and Sun and Moon".
March 1993
"Truganini" and "Bushfire" slightly different from album versions in intro and outro respectively.
"Truganini", "Bushfire" and "Drums Of Heaven" produced by Nick Launay & Midnight Oil. Recorded by Brent Clark & Nick Launay. Mixed by Nick Launay.
"Dreamworld", "Hercules", "Beds Are Burning", "Read About It", "Stars of Warburton" and "Only The Strong" (all live tracks) produced by Midnight Oil and Keith Walker. See credits to "Scream In Blue - Live" for further details.
