Notes: This CD comprises a mix of sources - the first 10 tracks come from the Oils Redneck Wonderland tour, the show itself being that broadcast on JJJ, recorded in Penrith (29/8/98).
The remaining 7 tracks are from a mix of sources but are all low quality reproductions. They are notable for featuring early versions of Beds, Bullroarer, In The Valley and Drums of Heaven, recorded before the actual album versions, and featuring slightly different arrangements and vocals. BFWF/TDH and You May Not Be Released come from the 1986 BFWF film shown on the original TV broadcast. Locomotive Breath is a short segment of the Jethro Tull song, performed by Pete, after being questioned on his audition for the band. As mentioned before the quality of the last 7 tracks is not great, but the rarity of the recordings more than makes up for that fact.
