Notes: This disc actually comprises two performances - tracks 1 to 11 originate from the Glastonbury 93 show (at the annual Glastonbury Festival), and the last three tracks come from a show in Flinders Park, Melbourne, 1993. The quality of recording is reasonably good and would appear to be sourced from a radio broadcast (listen for the "introduction" to Forgotten Years [track 9]). The performances themselves are good, featuring a decent span of mid-career Oils material.
