Unplugged Calgary, Electric Chicago
Other Names: None
Tracks: 11
Notes: No this isn't MTV Unplugged, it is from a Canadian Radioshow called Command Performance where they did 11 tracks including a small crack at Jethro Tull's Locomotive Breath which supposedly Pete did for his audition with the band. And tacked on are tracks 12-16 which are from the Tinley Park show. This is a really neat show like the Ronnie Scott's but the sets are pretty similar.
