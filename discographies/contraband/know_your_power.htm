Know Your Power
Other Names: Lumberjack
Tracks: 16
Notes: This is the show from Tinley Park's World Music Theater. August 7, 1993. It is a good show with 16 tracks with 3 pre-D&D tracks, 5 D&D tracks, 2 BSM tracks, and 6 ESM tracks. A good choice if you don't have anything recent from them. (Lumberjack is one track shorter, and Know You Power is one track longer)
