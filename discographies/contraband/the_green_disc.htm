The Green Disc
Other Names: None
Tracks:
  1. King of the Mountain
  2. Brave Faces
  3. No Time For Games
  4. The Dead Heart (Long Version)
  5. Kosciuszko
  6. Don't Wanna Be The One
  7. Power and the Passion (remix)
  8. No Reaction
  9. Powderworks
  10. Best Of Both Worlds
  11. Beds Are Burning (Tamarama Mix)
  12. Gunbarrel Highway
  13. Hercules
  14. Blue Sky Mine (Food On The Table Mix)
  15. Stand In Line (2JJ Live)
Notes: This disc is just a compilation of album tracks, kind of a Best Of compilation of the Oils only it has extended versions of The Dead Heart, Power and the Passion, Beds are Burning and Blue Sky Mine, along with the unreleased in the US, Gunbarrel Highway and Stand In Line. All in all it is 15 tracks long spanning every Oils album. While this is not technically a bootleg (it was released as a promo to radio stations) it is often lumped in with other bootlegs.
