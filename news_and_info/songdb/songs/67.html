General Song Notes: -
The song was originally called \"Kosciusko\" but was updated on the release of 20,000 Watt RSL.

Produced by Nick Launay and Midnight Oil. Engineered and Mixed by Nick Launay. Assistant Engineers - Shigeo Miyamoto, Yoshiyuki Kaneko. Recorded and mixed at Victor Aoyama Studio, Tokyo Jun-Aug 1984.
