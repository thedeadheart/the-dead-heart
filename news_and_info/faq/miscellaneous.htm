What is Strict Rules?
Strict Rules was a travelogue written by Andrew MacMillan, when he joined the Oils for their Black Fella / White Fella tour of the outback in 1986. Excerpts from the novel can be found at the official Midnight Oil site at http://www.midnightoil.com
