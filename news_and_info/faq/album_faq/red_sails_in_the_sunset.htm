Album FAQ - Red Sails in the Sunset

What is Red Sails in the Sunset?
RSITS is the bands 5th album.

Who produced Red Sails in the Sunset ?
RSITS was produced by Nick Launay and Midnight Oil.

When was Red Sails in the Sunset released?
RSITS was released in 1984.

Where was Red Sails in the Sunset recorded?
RSITS was recorded in Tokyo.

What singles were released from Red Sails in the Sunset?
Best of Both Worlds (and a When the Generals Talk promo).

Where did the title RSITS come from?
The original lyrics to Wedding Cake Island (Bird Noises EP) included the line "Red sails in the fucking sunset". Says Peter Garrett - "It's always been one of those phrases of ours; when we see something extraordinary, we'll go, 'Ahhh, red sails in the sunset'."
