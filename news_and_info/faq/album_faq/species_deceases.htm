Album FAQ - Species Deceases

What is Species Deceases?
SD is the name of the band's second EP.

Who produced Species Deceases?
SD was produced by Midnight Oil and Francois Kervorkian.

When was Species Deceases released?
SD was released in 1985.

Where was Species Deceases recorded?
SD was recorded in Paradise Studios in Darlinghurst, in five days during September (presumably 84).

Where did the title Species Deceases come from?
The title SD probably comes from the line "Don't want to be a member of a species that's deceasing" in the song Pictures from the EP.
