Album FAQ - Earth and Sun and Moon

What is Earth and Sun and Moon?
ESM is the bands 9th album.

Who produced Earth and Sun and Moon?
Earth and Sun and Moon was produced by Nick Launay .

When was Earth and Sun and Moon released?
ESM was released in 1993.

Where was Earth and Sun and Moon recorded?
ESM was recorded in Sydney.

What singles were released from Earth and Sun and Moon?
Truganini, My Country, Outbreak of Love and In the Valley were released.

Where did the title Earth and Sun and Moon come from?
ESM is the title of a song on the album Earth and Sun and Moon.

What are the mumbled words at the start of Earth and Sun and Moon?
"Blue ball of life, moon and the tide, earth is alive."
