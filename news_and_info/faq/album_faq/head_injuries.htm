Album FAQ - Head Injuries

What is Head Injuries?
HI is the title of the second album released by the band.

Where did the title Head Injuries come from?
One Powderworker heard a story that at an early gig, Pete was hit in the head by a projectile from the audience, possibly leading to the albums title!

Who produced Head Injuries?
HI was produced by Leszek Karski .

When was Head Injuries released?
HI was released in 1979.

What singles were released from Head Injuries?
Cold Cold Change and Back on the Borderline were released.

Where was Head Injuries recorded?
HI was recorded at Trafalgar Studios, Sydney.
