FAQs on This FAQ

Who created this FAQ?
This FAQ was created by Maurice Kelly , with large scale help from Pugwall (http://www.shef.ac.uk/students/gg/gga95jls/index.html) and Diana Lindsey.

Why was the FAQ created?
The FAQ was created due to the lack of an all-encompassing FAQ for Midnight Oil on the web. I decided that, due to the volume of repeated questions on the Powderworks mailing list, a better FAQ was needed.

When was this FAQ created?
This FAQ was created initially in March 97, but the current version may be drastically different from that which was originally created!
