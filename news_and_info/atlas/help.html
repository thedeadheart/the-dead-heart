Using The Atlas

Simply move the mouse pointer over the red dots to see the brief details for that area. Clicking on the dot will give more detailed information.
Use the buttons along the top to choose which of the two maps to use, or to close the atlas completely.

The atlas has been tested with Netscape 3, 4 and 4.5 and IE4 (all on PC).

Not working properly?
If you feel the atlas is not functioning properly (i.e. you get JavaScript errors) please do not hesitate to get in touch with The Dead Heart. The same advice applies if the data regarding locations is inaccurate!
