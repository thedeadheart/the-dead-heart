About The List

Powderworks has been running for quite a long time now. It is a usually unmoderated list that deals with Midnight Oil, the band members side projects, and related issues (within reason). The band are not present on the list, although they are aware of its existence. List members are located across the world with high concentrations in the US and Australia.

While the list is generally unmoderated, the administrator (Tim Hunter) does attempt to read every posting. If the subject matter is no longer on the right subject or discussion is getting too heated, he will moderate the list for a short period, and bounce any unsuitable posts (you have been warned!).

Newcomers are always welcome, but it is advised that before posting they consult both the Powderworks FAQ for list specific questions and the Midnight Oil FAQ here at The Dead Heart for Oils questions in general. This will save on repetitive posting, and could be the difference between making a graceful entry to Powderworks (appreciated) or crashing in with your feet in your mouth (not so much appreciated!). (Note that the Powderworks FAQ is available both here at The Dead Heart, and will be mailed to you when you join up!)
