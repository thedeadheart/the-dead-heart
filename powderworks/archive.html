The Powderowrks Archive has recently become unavailable, and so the Dead Heart would like to offer an alternative archive. The messages available here are copies of digests collected since around 1996. They've been split up into 6 volumes which have been zipped up to make downloading easier, but some are still up to 1.5Mb in size!

Please be aware that these zip files are not complete - Powderworks digests do not get sent out every day, so some numbers are missing. It's also the case that some digests may not have reached the digests over the years. Each zip file has two additional files - "subject list" and "results"; "subject list" should conatin a list of subject lines from each message for every digest in the zip file, and results should tell you what digest numbers are missing.
