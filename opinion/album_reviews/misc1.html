Black Rain Falls

In the All Music Guide

During the aftermath of the Exxon Valdez oilspill, Midnight Oil took it upon themselves to set up a lunch-hour protest concert, across the street from the oil company's headquarters in New York. What this performance lacks in technical perfection, it more than makes up for in passion. Included in this six-song set are versions of "Dreamworld," "Blue Sky Mine," and an impromptu sendup of John Lennon's "Instant Karma."

Reviewer: Rick Clark
