Redneck Wonderland (single)

In Time Off Magazine

The Oils are as politically-motivated as ever with this latest tune. In addition to the powerful messages, there is also some fine music here. The guitar line and backing track are fabulous, and the chorus is one of the catchier ones around at the moment. This song really illustrates the band's tremendous ability to change with the times. If you haven't already heard it on the radio, then you soon will.

Reviewer: Time Off Magazine
