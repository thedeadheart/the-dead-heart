Redneck Wonderland

Redneck Wonderland

Reviewed by Planet Sound (C4 Teletext) - UK

The phrase "new album from Midnight Oil" sends about as much excitement around the Planet Sound office as "free night of passion with Shane McGowan!"

But hey, its a new LP, and someone's gotta review it. As it is, the latest from this Oz-rock outfit (the title is a colloquial term for their homeland) sounds pretty much like their last.

The lyrics explore ecology, race, and there's a general concept of "postcards from a messed up island". Ho-hum.

Rated 2.5 (out of 5)

Reviewer: Stephen Eastwood
