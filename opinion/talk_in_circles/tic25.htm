TDH - Opinion - Bird Noises

  • "Kilo pascals in my finger" - as the Pascal is a measure of pressure, I guess kilo pascals signifies a lot of pressure. It's possible that Moginie wrote this to suggest that the finger has the power to apply a lot of pressure - either pointing the finger (eg in a court of law) or pehaps a finger poised over "the nuclear button"??

  • "God is hiding in this teacup" - could this refer to people looking for divine inspiration by that ancient art of reading tea-leaves?

  • The actual lyric that's sung is "God is hiding in this pickup" so, referring to playing guitar, it fits perfect with the bit about "kilopascals in my finger".

  • 'God', in the sense used by the lyricist here refers to salvation - in the form of heroin.
